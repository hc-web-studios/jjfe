Our Services

Stairs and balustrades

We design, fabricate, and install custom stairs and railings. Our work ranges from simple, functional stairways for industrial use to modern architectural balustrades for homes and offices. We work with various metals to match any style.

Marine Works and Repairs

We specialize in fabrication and repair for marine vessels and infrastructure. Our services include welding and repairing ship hulls, fabricating gangways, and creating other essential port equipment. Our expertise helps marine structures withstand harsh saltwater conditions.

Mining Component Fabrication

We fabricate durable, high-wear components for the mining industry. This includes items like chutes, hoppers, and conveyor systems. We use high-quality metals and specialized welding techniques to ensure our parts can handle the demanding conditions of mining operations.

Welding Processes & Materials

We are proficient in all major welding types: MIG, TIG, and ARC. We fabricate with a wide range of metals, including mild steel, stainless steel, copper, and brass, to suit any project's needs.
